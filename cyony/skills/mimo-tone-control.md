# MiMo TTS Tone Control (Discovery)

## Key Finding
MiMo TTS naturally adjusts vocal delivery based on emotional content of the text.

- Flat/casual text → casual delivery (Golden Corral voice)
- Low/intimate text → drops voice, intense delivery
- Sensory/physical words → gets breathy, slower
- Short sentences with pauses → naturally slows down
- ALL CAPS or exclamation → louder, more emphatic

## Implication
The WRITING is the temperature control. No need for Pocket's temperature slider to get mood variation from MiMo.

## How to Apply
- Write scene 3 (edge/control) content → MiMo sounds low and intense
- Write scene 1 (setup) content → MiMo sounds casual
- Use the Scene 3 Formula (control, sensory contrast, almost-touch, withholding) for ALL earbuds clips to get consistent intensity
