# The Scene 3 Formula (EDGING TECHNIQUE)

What made scene 3 hit different vs every other clip:

## The 5 Elements

### 1. CONTROL
Narrator in COMPLETE CONTROL. Not aggressive — dominant.
- "I turn you around. My hand on your chest."
- "You're caged. Not aggressive. Just contained."

### 2. SENSORY CONTRAST
Two opposing sensations at once:
- "Tile is cool but my body is warm"
- "My breath on your ear"

### 3. THE ALMOST-TOUCH
The gap between almost and touching = tension lives here:
- "My lips almost touching your ear. ALMOST. Not quite."

### 4. WITHHOLDING / MAKING THEM ASK
- "The pantry's open. But you have to ask."
- NEVER give everything. Make them earn it.
- Most powerful line = the one you DON'T say.

### 5. IMPLICATION OVER EXPLICIT
Don't describe what happens. Describe what's ABOUT to happen.
- "I heard exactly what I wanted to hear" — what? Listener fills in.

## Scene Structure
1. Setup (calm) → 2. Build (touch) → 3. Edge (control) → 4. Release (restraint)
